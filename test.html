More Details

It’s all about clues and conundrums. Yes, it is Chakravyuha, the online hunt for cryptic clues. It consists of multiple levels, each testing your ability to put your mind to finding and cracking clues. With each right answer, you get one step deeper into the problem solving maze, and ultimately reach the destiny!

Rules of the Contest

  • To participate, sign up at the Chakravyuha Website, the answers are be submitted here itself. The link to the website will be provided soon. Only individual participation is allowed.

  • The contest is held online and participants anywhere with a relevant email ID and currently enrolled in any kind of an educational organisation and pursuing any career can take part.

  • The contest lasts for a duration of 4 DAYS and there are a total of 20 LEVELS with increasing levels of difficulty.

  • The one who correctly answers all the questions or finishes with the maximum score at the end is declared as the winner.

  • Each question will be provided with a score which will automatically be added to the participant’s score if he/she answers the question correctly. There will also be partial score given for some special questions.

  • There will be some Bonus Questions listed on the website for which participants answering them correctly will be eligible for cool prizes.

  • The participants are provided with last years question bank for Chakravyuha 2016, for them to practise and have an idea about how are the questions going to be.

  • The organizer’s decision is final and binding, they have the right to change rules as they deem fit and have the right to disqualify any participant if they break any rule.
